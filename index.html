About the Game

Chaos ball is a red ball inspired platformer game, it was developed by a group of 4 developers at GITAM university for the Shore Fest'26 Vibe Coding Hackathon. Chaos Ball has a special mechanic that fundamentally changes the physics upon losing, bringing new challenges for players. The game was made with assistance with Claude AI.
